Indian Applicants

Indian citizens can gain entry as spouses and dependents of E2 visa applicants or can immigrate under the E2 visa if they can procure an ‘in-treaty’ nationality.

Although it is not possible to gain citizenship based on long term residence in countries like the UK, Australia, Germany, and Canada, there is the option of quickly gaining citizenship, in a Caribbean Country.

Carribean citizenship programs are high-speed and do not require residency. They are very convenient for Indians and others who need another citizenship to be eligible to immigrate to the USA via the E2 visa.

For example, Grenada citizenship is permitted for the E2 Treaty Investor visa. An applicant who has gained Grenada citizenship can be allowed US entry for the E2 investor and family members for an initial five year period and then can be extended indefinitely.
